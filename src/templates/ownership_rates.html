Q/A
What are EP values?

These values are 'expected points' reported by Official FPL API. It is simply a Moving Average stat of last few game points, shown here to give an idea on ownership trends.

The information contained on this website is for general information purposes only. Any reliance you place on this information is strictly at your own risk.
